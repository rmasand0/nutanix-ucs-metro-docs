# Glossary of Terms

* **AOS (Acropolis Operating System)**: The core software service for Nutanix clusters.
* **CIMC (Cisco Integrated Management Controller)**: The management interface for standalone Cisco servers.
* **Consistency Group**: A group of VMs that are replicated together to maintain write-order fidelity.
* **IMM (Intersight Managed Mode)**: Modern Cisco management using policy-driven templates and domain profiles.
* **ISM (Intersight Standalone Mode)**: Direct Intersight management of servers without Fabric Interconnect requirements.
* **Metro Availability**: Continuous data protection via synchronous replication between two sites with zero RPO.
