# 2-Day Workshop Agenda: Nutanix Metro on Cisco UCS

## Day 1: Architecture & Foundations
* **09:00 - 10:30**: **Module 1: Introduction** - Business cases for Metro & Cisco Management modes.
* **10:45 - 12:30**: **Module 2: Metro Overview** - Sync vs. Async and the role of the Witness.
* **13:30 - 15:00**: **Module 3: Architecture Deep Dive** - Synchronous I/O flow and failure domains.
* **15:15 - 17:00**: **Module 4: Intersight Standalone (ISM)** - Hardware preparation and CIMC staging.

## Day 2: Implementation & Operations
* **09:00 - 11:00**: **Module 5: AOS Installation** - Workflow for IMM, ISM, and UCSM.
* **11:15 - 12:30**: **Module 6: Network Design** - vPC, MTU 9000, and ISL latency requirements.
* **13:30 - 15:00**: **Module 7: Metro Deployment** - Cluster pairing and Protection Domains.
* **15:15 - 16:30**: **Module 8: Operations** - Failover testing and Lifecycle Management.
* **16:30 - 17:00**: **Workshop Wrap-up** - Final Q&A and Resource Review.
